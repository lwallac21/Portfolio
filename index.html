Lawrence Wallace

Musician, Dad, and Coding Beginner,

Lawrence Wallace

Lawrence is a musician and aspiring coder, with a deep and abiding interest in medieval history, literature, video games and food. Lawrence has done Opera for the Mozartfest in Salzburg, Austria, and he has directed music for many plays, including Shrek with Near West Theatre, Batboy with Blank Canvas Theatre, and 13! with Beachwood Community Theatre. He was born in 1986 and is the oldest of four children. He lives with his wife Julia and his two children (another one on the way!) Ben and Evie.

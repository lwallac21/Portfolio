My Portfolio

My Wings

is part of Lawrence's play Paradise Lost that tells the story of the John Milton's eponymous epic poem. The play follows four characters, Lucifer (the Devil), Michael (The Archangel), Adam (the first man), and Eve (the First Woman). The play takes a different approach from the biblical narrative, casting Lucifer as more of a heroic figure, and everyone making good faith decisions. Each of the characters are driven by the weaknesses inherited after their creation, and ultimately they each make sacrifices to achieve their various goals. In this particular song, Lucifer sings about his decision to leave heaven, and how he can still be righteous, even without the approval of God. The song takes place on Earth, and it is the Act I finale.

EOC

is the title of an art show devised by Bili Kribbs, a very talented artist in the Cleveland area. He asked Lawrence to create an ambient, looping soundtrack to serve as background music for the show. The mood evoked by the show was to be earthy and a little alien, to show off the toys, paintings, and other media Bili created for the event. Recorded using Native Instrument's Thrill VST plugin and the MassiveX and Serum Synth VSTs.

Gloria

is one of the very many pieces Lawrence has composed and performed in his role as the music director for Saint'Luke catholic church in Lakewood, Ohio. This piece is a clip of the Gloria from his Mass for the Sick, which he composed in memory of the victims of Coronavirus in the parish.
